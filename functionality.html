Functionality

Create New Meeting

On the main screen there is a button which will allow you to create a new whiteboard meeting. It will take you a screen where you can specify the date, start time and end time for the meeting you want to create. It will also allow you to invite people to your meeting; these invitees will receive notification/a reminder via email.

Whiteboard

Once you have entered your meeting PIN you will be taken to a screen with a canvas. From here you can draw rectangles, draw characters, edit the size, position and rotation of drawn objects and clear the screen.
